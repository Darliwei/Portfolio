What i do

UI Design

Elevating digital experiences through the art of crafting visually captivating and intuitively navigable user interfaces. I specialize in shaping digital canvases that not only catch the eye but also serve as seamless bridges between users and their goals, transforming mere interactions into memorable journeys.

User Research

Illuminating the design path with deep insights and precision. Our passion lies in diving into the minds and experiences of users, dissecting their needs, behaviors, and desires, by conducting meticulous user studies and analysis, we paint a vivid portrait of your audience.

HTML CSS Development

As a UX engineer, I serve as the bridge between design and engineering, harnessing the dynamic duo of HTML and CSS to craft captivating, user-centric web interfaces. I dont only build components but also diligently manage and document code.

Interaction Design

I specialize in creating engaging and user-friendly digital experiences. I use a mix of gestures, clicks, and swipes to craft compelling stories that guide users on exciting journeys. My goal is simple, make interactions enjoyable and easy, turning every moment into a memorable and delightful experience.

Design Systems Management

I create and maintain design systems, ensuring every element harmonizes seamlessly. In this symphony of creativity, every pixel, font, and color contributes to a harmonious whole, I also apply the 'atomic design' method, using tiny building blocks to create cohesive and efficient designs.

SEO Optimization

I specialize in SEO optimization, which means I work to improve website visibility and rankings on search engines. I create strategies, optimize content, and target keywords to increase organic traffic and online success.
